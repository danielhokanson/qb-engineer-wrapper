W-4: Federal Tax Withholding

How to complete this form

Filing Status (Step 1c): Choose the status that best describes your situation. This is the most important field and affects every paycheck.

Multiple Jobs (Step 2): Check this if you (or your spouse) hold more than one job simultaneously. This prevents under-withholding.

Dependents (Step 3): Enter the number of qualifying children under 17 (line 3a, worth $2,000 each) and other dependents (line 3b, worth $500 each). Most employees leave both at 0.

Other Adjustments (Step 4 — Optional): Use these fields to fine-tune withholding for investment income, itemized deductions, or extra withholding per paycheck.

Exempt: Only claim exempt if you owed $0 in federal tax last year and expect to owe $0 this year. Most employees do not qualify.
